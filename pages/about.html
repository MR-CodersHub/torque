Our Mission

Driving the Automotive Revolution

We blend state-of-the-art software systems with master mechanical tuning to deliver sustainable and high-performing automotive transformations.

Who We Are

Founded by a group of engineers, programmers, and custom auto designers, Torque EV bridges the gap between mechanical heritage and cloud computing. We believe that cars shouldn't just be transportation; they should be high-performance, intelligent extensions of our digital lives.

Whether converting custom internal combustion engines into high-capacity electric drivetrains or customizing smart dashboards that run machine learning models at the edge, our goal is to push the boundaries of mechanical possibility.

Our Vision

To make high-performance electric power and deep software integration accessible to every car enthusiast and logistics fleet worldwide.

Core Values

Craftsmanship, software innovation, energy efficiency, and high fidelity aesthetic values in every weld and line of code.
