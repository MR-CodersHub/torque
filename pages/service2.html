Retrofitting Walkthrough

We execute conversions inside custom clean rooms through a structured stage timeline:

  1. 3D Scanning: Sub-millimeter digital scanning of your engine compartment.
  2. CAD Design: Designing custom structural brackets and battery racks.
  3. Mechanical Integration: Extracting mechanical drivetrain, mounting EV components.
  4. Calibration & Dyno: Commissioning telemetry scripts, calibrating traction control.
